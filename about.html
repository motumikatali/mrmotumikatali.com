ABOUT ME

I am a proud Mosotho man, aged 22. I was born in 2000 June 16, on a drizzly friday, in Mapoteng. I grew up in a cosy village named Community in Botha Bothe. I was given a privellage to attend school at Soofia International School up until grade 12. Now, I am a university student at Limkonkwing.


As a person, I believe life is an art, and I am the artist of my life. I try to keep it full of colors and as cheerful as possible no matter what life throws at me. I am an enthusiast and a person full of curiosity. Curious to know what life is all about and what it holds in the future.
